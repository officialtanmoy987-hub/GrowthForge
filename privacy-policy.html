GrowthForge processes your data based on legitimate business interests, contractual fulfillment, and legal compliance. We use this information to:

  • Maintain security, detect malicious activity, and protect against fraud.
cookie

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember preferences and analyze traffic. We use the following types of cookies:

• Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website, enabling us to optimize performance (e.g. Google Analytics).

• Functionality Cookies: Remember settings and customization choices you make (such as visual preferences or forms filled).

• Management: You can manage, disable, or clear cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain interactive elements of the website.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to keep your personal data secure. This includes:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) certificates for all public domains.
  • Secure hosting environments on top-tier global infrastructure.
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) and access control filters for databases.
  • Restricting database access strictly to authorized personnel.
  • Regular application patches, platform updates, and compliance audits.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you hold legal rights regarding your personal information, which you can exercise at any time:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data held by us.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: The right to request erasure of your data under specific conditions.
  • Objection/Consent Withdrawal: The right to withdraw marketing consent or object to data processing.
  • Portability: The right to request the transfer of your data to another provider.

9. Children's Privacy

Our websites, agency services, and digital consultations are not structured for or directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under 13 years of age. If you believe we have accidentally collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately to have it deleted.
